MIYERKULET

MiyerKULET! is a regular Wednesday event, providing a safe space for the arts and embracing the belief that arts are a crucial aspect of human existence. It serves as a consistent virtual and physical platform for artists, filmmakers, musicians, and creators to freely present and discuss their work without financial constraints.

SINESYUDAD

Sine Siyudad is a community-based film screening event organized in collaboration with local stakeholders, organizations, and institutions. Its main objective is to stimulate meaningful discussions and reflections among the audience by showcasing thought-provoking films within the context of Philippine social realities and human rights.

HRPEC CHIKAHAN

HRPEC Chikahan is a monthly roundtable discussion that unites diverse participants, including experts, activists, policymakers, and community representatives, to tackle pressing human rights issues. This inclusive platform fosters open and constructive dialogue, enabling the exchange of ideas, experiences, and perspectives on advancing human rights practices.

HR DISCUSSION

HRPEC's Human Rights Education lectures and seminars have been instrumental in achieving the organization's objectives, reaching over 170 venues. Recognizing the importance of early awareness and education on human rights, HRPEC believes that such knowledge fosters a lifelong commitment to upholding innate rights. For the past five years, HRPEC has been providing these lectures and seminars at little to no cost, engaging audiences in discussions about human rights in the Philippines. Today, HRPEC's Human Rights Discussions prioritize a listener's perspective and acknowledge the growing awareness of individual rights and their impact on local communities.
